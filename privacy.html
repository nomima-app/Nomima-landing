Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately

The short version
  • Your notes never leave your Mac. Everything is stored in a local SQLite database inside your vault folder.
  • We don't collect analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or usage data. We have no visibility into what you write.
  • No account required. No email address needed to use the app.
  • AI keys are stored in your macOS Keychain — we never see them. AI calls go directly from your machine to your chosen provider.
  • If you buy Pro, Lemon Squeezy processes the payment. We only receive a licence key status signal — no card details, ever.

1. Who we are

Nomima is a macOS application developed by the Nomima team. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to Nomima and its developers. You can reach us at support@nomima.io.

2. What Nomima stores — and where

Nomima is a local-first, offline application. All data it creates is stored entirely on your Mac:

  • Your notes, blocks, tags, links, and attachments are stored in a SQLite database inside your vault folder (a directory you choose).
  • Embedded media files (audio, video, images) are stored in a media/ subfolder inside your vault.
  • AI provider keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, or local endpoints) are stored in your macOS Keychain, not in any file we can access.
  • Licensing state (trial start date, licence key validation cache) is stored in a small local file and your macOS Keychain.
  • Application preferences (theme, sound settings, etc.) are stored in macOS UserDefaults.

None of this data is transmitted to our servers. We have no backend that receives your notes or personal content.

3. Data we do not collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • The content of your notes, tags, or any text you type.
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or feature telemetry.
  • Crash reports or error logs sent to our servers.
  • Your IP address or location.
  • Device identifiers beyond what is needed for licence validation (see §5).

4. Network requests the app makes

Nomima only initiates outbound network requests in the following situations:

  • Licence validation — if you activate a Pro licence key, the app contacts the Lemon Squeezy API to confirm your key is valid. This check uses your licence key and a stable machine identifier only. No note content is included.
  • Bookmark metadata — when you paste a URL into a Bookmark block, the app fetches the page's <meta> tags (title, description, image) from that URL. This is a direct request from your Mac to the target website — it does not pass through our servers.
  • RSS feeds — when you use the RSS Feed block, the app fetches the feed URL directly from your Mac. No proxy is involved.
  • AI providers — if you use the AI Chat or /Generate features, your messages are sent directly from your Mac to the provider you chose (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, or a local server). Nomima does not relay or log these requests.
  • MCP server — the built-in MCP server listens on local loopback (127.0.0.1) only and is not accessible from the internet.

5. Licensing and payments

Nomima offers a free tier with a paid Pro upgrade. If you purchase Pro:

  • Payment is processed by Lemon Squeezy (lemonsqueezy.com), a third-party payment processor. We never receive your card number, billing address, or full payment details.
  • After purchase you receive a licence key. The app periodically contacts Lemon Squeezy's validation endpoint to confirm the key is still active. This call includes only your licence key and a non-personal machine identifier (a hash of your Mac's hardware UUID). No content data is included.
  • Lemon Squeezy's own privacy policy governs how they handle payment information: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

During the beta period, no payment is required and no licence checks occur.

6. Third-party AI providers

The AI Chat and /Generate features are bring-your-own-key. You supply the API key; the app stores it in your macOS Keychain and sends requests directly to your chosen provider. We do not proxy, store, or inspect these requests.

Each provider has its own data processing terms:

  • Anthropic — anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI — openai.com/privacy
  • Google — policies.google.com/privacy

If you use a local model (Ollama, LM Studio, etc.) no data leaves your machine at all.

7. Website and contact form

This website (nomima.io) does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts. If you submit the contact form, your name, email address, chosen topic, and message are sent to support@nomima.io via FormSubmit (formsubmit.co). We retain that information only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry.

If you sign up for the waiting list, your email is stored with Mailchimp and governed by their privacy policy. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

8. Children's privacy

Nomima is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information via our contact form, please email us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app's features or applicable law. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes and at the top of this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of Nomima after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
