Privacy

No cloud. No tracking.
No compromise.

Nomima is local-first by design. Your vault is a plain SQLite database on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is sent to a server.

Full-text search

SQLite FTS5 — instant, local, private. No index leaves your device.

Semantic search

On-device embeddings find related notes even when the keywords don't match.

Entity recognition

People, companies, places, and technologies extracted on-device — no API calls.

No accounts

No sign-up, no login, no telemetry. There are no subscriptions and no analytics.

Media vault

Imported files, images, and audio live inside your vault directory — portable and self-contained.

Works offline

Move or copy your vault folder and everything comes with it. No internet required, ever.

Export

Your notes, anywhere
you need them.

Everything in Nomima lives locally — and can leave whenever you want. Export any note to PDF in one click, or share it to any app on your Mac via the native Share Sheet.

PDF Export

One click — File → Export PDF. Rendered natively by macOS. Clean pagination, proper fonts, print-ready output. Fully offline.

Share Sheet

Export to any app on your Mac — AirDrop, Mail, Notes, Messages — via the native macOS Share Sheet.

Local vault

Your SQLite vault lives in App Support — back it up, move it, or open it directly. Your data, no lock-in.

Images & attachments

Drag files into any note — they're stored in your vault alongside your writing. Export the whole folder as-is.
