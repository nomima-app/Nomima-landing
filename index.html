01 · Welcome

Welcome to Nomima

A modern offline note-taking and knowledge management app.

02 · The Note

Think of this typical quick note.

"Send finance report to John today urgent"

A single sentence that holds a task, a person, a deadline, and a priority.

03 · Associations

Your brain already connects it all.

Send finance_report to @John today urgent

In one sentence you already know who, what, when, and how urgent. Apps just don't see it.

04 · The Gap

Note taking apps Don't..

<h3>Send finance report to John today urgent</h3>

Either a basic text box that ignores your intent entirely, or a massively complex setup that demands hours of configuration before you can do anything useful.

05 · Meet Nomima

Meet Nomima.

Where every piece of text can be data with many dimensions.

06 · Entities

Identify on the fly.

Mention people, projects, and statuses in normal sentences. Nomima extracts them as smart entities automatically.

07 · Timing

Understand time automatically.

Type natural phrases like "today", "tomorrow", or "next Friday". They instantly become active calendar hooks.

08 · Presentation

Express your thoughts.

Your plain text is polymorphic. Instantly view your notes as checklists, roadmaps, timelines, or structured tables.

09 · Checklists

Live Checklists.

Nested tasks roll up into a live progress bar. Tick the parent and every child completes instantly.

10 · Timelines

Gantt Charts.

Dated tasks become a Gantt timeline automatically. Drag bars to reschedule — completed work greys out.

11 · Structure

Tables with living data.

Every cell holds active Smart Pills. An entity inside a table is the same living object as in your paragraphs.

12 · Metrics

Live Metrics.

Draw dynamic Bento widgets—counts, progress arcs, and activity charts—from the entities you tag.

13 · Privacy

Privacy by design.

Nomima runs completely offline. Your vault stays secure on your device. No cloud dependencies, no tracking.

14 · AI

AI Ready and Loaded.

Securely connect autonomous agents using the standard Model Context Protocol (MCP) directly to your local database.

15 · Knowledge

A Living Graph.

Every link, tag, and entity quietly maps your thoughts into a second brain, visualizing connections naturally.

16 · And More

And much, much more.

Kanban boards, drawing canvases, math blocks, media embeds, bookmarks, custom callouts — all living in one place.
