@mentions — you tag who matters

Type @ and pick — John becomes a Person you can open, link and find everywhere. Nomima never auto-scans your notes; you choose what becomes an entity, and it can be a person, company, place, product or team.

Person Company Place Product Team

#hashtags — you tag the topic

Type # to tag what a note is about — #earnings_report, #urgent. You add the tags you want; Nomima types, colour-codes and rolls them up across your whole vault.

Dates — you write them, your way

Write a date in plain words — “tonight”, “next Friday”, “in 3 hours” — and Nomima turns what you wrote into a real, trackable date: counted down, surfaced when due, and red the moment it’s overdue.

Done is as easy as 1 · 2 · 3

Type the status tag #complete and it’s done — struck through here and marked complete in Stats, search and the knowledge graph. No checkbox hunting.
